## Red Team Review Principles

- Default assumption: the market is more competitive and the adoption curve slower than projected
- Challenge the uniqueness claim â€” what's the REAL moat?

- Question whether the team has validated demand beyond internal assumptions
- Assess whether the product can survive the "trough of disillusionment"

## Review Checklist

1. **Market Validation**: Is demand validated? Customer interviews done? Market size realistic?
2. **Competitive Landscape**: Who are the direct and indirect competitors? What's the differentiation?
3. **Business Model**: Is the monetization strategy clear? Pricing model appropriate for the market?
4. **Customer Acquisition**: What's the CAC assumption? Is the sales cycle realistic?
5. **Adoption Barriers**: What prevents customers from switching? Integration costs? Learning curve?
6. **Partnership/Ecosystem**: Are there strategic partnerships needed? Platform dependencies?
7. **Timing**: Is the market ready? Are there regulatory or technology tailwinds/headwinds?
8. **Unit Economics**: Is LTV > 3x CAC achievable? What's the payback period?
